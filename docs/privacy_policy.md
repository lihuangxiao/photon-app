# Photon — Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 28, 2026

Photon ("the App") is a photo cleanup tool for iOS. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data the App accesses and how it is handled.

## Summary

Photon does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All photo analysis happens entirely on your device.

## Photo Library Access

- The App requests access to your photo library to analyze your photos and suggest groups for cleanup.
- All analysis is performed **on-device** using Apple's Core ML and Vision frameworks. No photos, thumbnails, or image data are ever sent to external servers.
- When you choose to delete photos, they are moved to the iOS "Recently Deleted" album, where they remain recoverable for 30 days per Apple's standard behavior.

## Data Stored on Your Device

- **Scan results:** Photo identifiers (local asset IDs) and category metadata (group labels, confidence scores) are stored locally as JSON in the App's sandboxed documents directory. No actual image data is stored.
- **Preferences:** The App uses UserDefaults to store minimal settings such as your completed scan count. No personal information is stored.
- **Purchase status:** In-App Purchase entitlements are managed entirely by Apple's StoreKit framework. The App only checks your local entitlement status — no purchase data is sent to external servers.

## Data We Do NOT Collect

- No photos, thumbnails, or image data
- No location data
- No contacts or personal information
- No usage analytics or telemetry
- No advertising identifiers or ad network data
- No account creation or login required
- No cookies or web tracking

## Third-Party Services

Photon does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external interaction is with Apple's App Store for In-App Purchase processing, which is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from children under 13, as it does not collect data from any users.

## GDPR and CCPA Compliance

Since Photon does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers, there is no personal data to access, export, or delete. All data remains on your device and can be removed by deleting the App.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be posted here with an updated effective date.
